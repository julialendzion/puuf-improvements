    CIRCULARITY

    Round & round it goes!

    It’s one thing to simply identify a problem, and another thing to actively engage with it. Since we started PUUF back in 2017 we have always sought to experiment with circularity and sustainability. Understanding it, educating ourselves and others on it with the aim to implement it into all our processes, and throughout. Working sustainably has become our passion, our focus and strategy and is based on 4 pillars.

    Act local, think global!

    When we design, create, buy, our aim is to always go for the most environmentally responsible solution. These practices directly correspond to SDGs number 8, 12 and 13. We bring the UN’s 17 SDGs close to our daily work, because even the smallest, local actions have global consequences.
